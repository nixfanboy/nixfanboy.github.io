White Russian

Preparation: In a mixing glass, combine the coffee flavored liqueur, vodka and cream or milk. Pour over ice in a highball glass.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Kahlúa
  • 1 Splash of Heavy Cream

Fun Fact: The White Russian cocktail was conceived in 1949 when Gustave Tops, a Belgian barman, created the cocktail, along with its sister cocktail, the black Russian – a White Russian without any cream.
