Kamikaze

Preparation: Makes 2 shots. Add all the ingredients into a shaker with ice, and shake thoroughly until well-chilled. Strain into two shot glasses.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 3/4 oz Orange liqueur
  • 3/4 oz Fresh lime juice
