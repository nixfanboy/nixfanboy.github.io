Pina Colada

Preparation: The ice, pineapple, juice, cream, and rum are blended until smooth. Pour into glass and garnish rim with pineapple slices.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Frozen Pineapple
  • 1 cup Ice
  • 2 oz. Coconut Cream
  • 2 oz. Pineapple Juice
  • 1 oz. White Rum
  • 1 oz. Dark Rum
  • Pineapple Slice (garnish)

Fun Fact: a story from the 19th century states that a pirate by the name of Roberto Cofresi served a coconut-pineapple drink to his crew to boost morale. However, this origin has been disputed over the years by historians.
